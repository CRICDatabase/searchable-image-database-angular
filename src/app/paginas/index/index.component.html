CRIC Searchable Image Database

CRIC Searchable Image Database is a public cervical cell image database aiming supporting cervical cancer analysis of Pap smear. We have collected hundreds of hand labeled segmentation and classification of cervical cells from 4 (four) human specialists (pathologists). The database is a collaborative work among Federal University of Ouro Preto, Federal University of Ceará and University of California-Berkeley.

We hope CRIC Searchable Image Database will become a useful resource for cervical cancer investigation and may help physicians to provide a healthier life for women worldwide.
