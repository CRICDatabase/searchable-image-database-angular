About CRIC Searchable Image Database

Center for Recognition and Inspection of Cells (CRIC) Searchable Image Database is one of the products from a multidisciplinary team of researchers from computer science and biology.

The database is a collection of real images representing a range of different lesions in cells interpreted by independent experts. The database has information about image diagnostic and individual cell classification.

CRIC Searchable Image Database Collections

More collections will be added in the future.
